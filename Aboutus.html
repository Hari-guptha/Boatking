Who are we?

Welcome to Pondy Kings Boating! We proudly serve four picturesque locations: Mangrove, Pondy Fishing Harbors, Arikimedu, and River Mouyh. Our mission is to provide unforgettable boating experiences, whether you're looking for a peaceful day on the water or an adventure. Join us at any of our beautiful destinations for an exceptional boating experience.

License Details

We are approved by the Tourism Department of Pondicherry and have been operating since 2023.
